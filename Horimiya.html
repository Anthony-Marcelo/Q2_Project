Next up, a very heart-warming rom-com, Horimiya!

Horimiya is a very cute romantic comedy anime focused around two star-crossed lovers: Hori, and Miyamura. Unlike other rom-coms, it rarely relies on tropes and reused (maybe overused) scenarios, and instead, relies on unusual but realistic happenings to make the viewer laugh. It's also different from most rom-coms because the romantic aspect of the anime is given out in equal, if not more in measurement compared to its comedic side. Aside from that, its love story is also very believable in terms of how its couple fell in love with each other using the friends to lovers trope.

For the destination we're focusing on in this anime, we must fast-forward to Season 2 (Horimiya: The Missing Pieces) Episode 1 wherein they head off to Kyoto for their class trip.
