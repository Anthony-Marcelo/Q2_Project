  • What'll go show up in Photo Albums?
    • When you click Photo Album Titles in the format section of the Table of Contents, this will show back up.
    • When you click the Photo Album Titles of any named anime and location, expect it to show:
      • The title of the Photo Album
      • Pictures of the location in real life (that looks the closest to the one in the anime shot)
      • Essay-like content (again)
        1. A "Did you know?" line
        2. Fun facts/Trivia about the location chosen
        3. Location's relation to the anime (and how it's referenced to in the anime)
        4. How well the anime potrays the given locations
      • References
